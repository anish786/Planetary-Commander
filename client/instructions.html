Summary

1. Hit Play to connect to the server.
2. You get 1 movement from each planet you own per turn.
3. Click on source planet (owned/blue) then destination (adjacent) to make a movement with the current percent.
4. 0-9 keys or mouse scroll wheel change current percent.
5. You can only see information regarding planets that are directly adjacent to yours
6. The Game ends when one player no longer owns any planet, or if a player disconnects.

Tips:

1. The larger the planet the more units it will take to initially take control.
2. After you have owned a planet for a turn it will begin generating extra troops based on its size.
      Large Planets give 10 per turn
      Medium Planets give 5 per turn
      Small Planets give 2 per turn

INSTRUCTIONS

To connect to the server hit the play button.




While you are waiting for the server to match you with another player you will see this waiting message.



When the game starts you will be on a random start planet with 50 units. The start planet is always a large planet.



At the start of your turn you have 3 minutes. Your turn will end when the three minutes expire or you hit the Finalize button.


Time remaining is shown in the bottom middle.


For each planet you own you can make 1 movement per turn.
To control the percent of units you send from a planet use the 0-9 keys or mouse scroll wheels on your keyboard.


The current percent to send is shown in the bottom left corner.


To make a movement first click on the source planet.




Then click on the destination planet.




By clicking on the units that appear between the source and destination planet you can cancel a movement allowing you to remake a movement from that planet or not perform one at all.



After you are done making all your movements and hit finish, or if the time expires you will then need to wait for you opponent to finalize their movements or for the timer to expire.



At this point the map will update and the next turn begins.
