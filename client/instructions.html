RULES OF PLAY

You will move around your troops between planets in a sequence of turns.

You will have 3 minutes to finalize a move.

After each turn: large planets will increase +10, medium planets will increase +5, and small planets will increase +2.

Large:                       Medium:                       Small:

          +10                 +5                 +2

HOW TO PLAY
